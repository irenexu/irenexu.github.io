Course Content and Learning Expectations:
Economics ideas are simple and yet have extraordinary power to explain, predict and improve what happens in the world.
In this course students will learn some basic tools used in economic analysis.
In the first part of the course you will learn the underpinnings of the demand and supply curve, so where the curves actually come from.
At the core of demand and supply curves is the concept of optimization: the buyer and the seller try to choose the best available options.
The buyer decides what to buy, given her preferences, the prices and the available budget.
